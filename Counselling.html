Dr.B.Ravishankar is a proficient psychiatrist graduated from Christian Medical College, Vellore. He has been practicing for 21 years in psychiatry and psychology. He treats a wide range of mental disorders across different age groups and excels in treatment of psychological issues in medically ill patients.
